🧠 Vizancia

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 31, 2026

🔒 Zero Data Collection

Overview

Vizancia is an offline-first educational app that teaches Artificial Intelligence concepts through interactive lessons and mini-games. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Collect

None. Vizancia does not collect any personal information whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No email addresses, names, or contact information collected
  • No location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

Vizancia stores your learning progress locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Lesson completion progress and scores
  • XP earned, level, and streak data
  • Achievement unlock status
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

Vizancia does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection SDKs. The app functions entirely offline. The only external service used is Apple's Game Center, which powers optional multiplayer features (available to high school grade band only). Game Center is managed entirely by Apple under their own privacy policy. Vizancia does not access, store, or transmit your Game Center profile information.

Children's Privacy

Vizancia does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Notifications

If you choose to enable daily reminder notifications, these are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification system. No data is sent to any server.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes here with a new effective date. Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing material to change.
